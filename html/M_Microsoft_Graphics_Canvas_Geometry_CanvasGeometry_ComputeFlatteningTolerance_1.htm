Remarks
This computation guarantees that under any scaling up to the maximum zoom level, curves will have no loss of fidelity. Maximum zoom level is the highest scale factor that the app anticipates applying to the geometry, independent of the geometry transform. For more information, see ComputeFlatteningTolerance.
